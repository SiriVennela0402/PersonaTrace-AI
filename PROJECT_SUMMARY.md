# PersonaTrace AI Project Summary

## Title

PersonaTrace AI: AI-Based Deepfake Interview Risk Detection System

## One-Line Pitch

PersonaTrace AI analyzes remote interview videos and produces an explainable risk score for possible deepfake or identity manipulation signals.

## What It Checks

- Face consistency across sampled frames
- Missing face or multiple faces
- Blink and eye-signal behavior
- Frozen or unusually static face motion
- Speech activity versus mouth-region movement
- Video blur, lighting variation, and abrupt frame glitches

## Output

- Interview Risk Score
- Risk Level
- Key findings
- Evidence frames
- Module-level risk signals

## Resume Description

Built PersonaTrace AI, a Streamlit and OpenCV-based deepfake interview risk screening system for remote hiring. The system extracts video frames and audio, analyzes face consistency, blink/motion behavior, speech-mouth timing, and video quality artifacts, then produces an explainable risk score with supporting reasons.
